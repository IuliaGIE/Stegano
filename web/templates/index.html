Welcome to the Steganography App!

Steganography is the practice of hiding information within another medium (which is, usually, communicated in plain

sight). If in encryption the difficulty lies in finding the secret (i.e.: the key) used to obfuscate the data, here the

problem consists of detecting whether the data exists at all (the plausible deniability concept).
